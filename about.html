Meet Eli Chanimov

Welcome to Eli's Barbershop - Toronto's trusted gents salon. Owner and head barber Eli Chanimov brings years of experience and a genuine passion for the craft to every cut.

Located at 4128 Bathurst St in the heart of Toronto, we've built a reputation for exceptional service, fair pricing, and a relaxed atmosphere where every client feels at home.

Whether you're coming in for a fresh fade, a classic cut, or a beard trim, we take the time to understand what you want and deliver results you'll love.

We are a Shomer Shabbat establishment - closed on Saturdays and Jewish holidays.
